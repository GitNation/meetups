[React Amsterdam](https://guild.host/react-amsterdam) is a community focused on all things React, from local meetup nights to the wider React Summit ecosystem. It brings together front-end engineers in Amsterdam for practical talks, demos, and shared experience from the React world.

## Sponsoring

Companies can support React Amsterdam by hosting the meetup or by sponsoring food and drinks for the audience.

### Sponsorship Options

- Venue hosting: provide the space for the meetup
- Food and beverages: sponsor snacks, drinks, or both

### Venue Requirements

- An accessible Amsterdam location that works well for an evening meetup
- Enough room for talks, audience seating, and mingling before or after the program
- Basic A/V support for presentations and demos

### Benefits

- Brand placement on the event page and meetup materials
- A short company intro during the event
- Direct face time with attendees before, during, and after the talks
- A practical way to support the Amsterdam React community and meet local engineers
