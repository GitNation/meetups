[AmsterdamJS](https://guild.host/amsterdamjs) is the largest and longest-running JavaScript meetup in town. Since 2010 it has brought together the Amsterdam JavaScript community for practical talks, demos, experiments, and real-world lessons from across the ecosystem.

## Sponsoring

Companies can support AmsterdamJS by hosting the meetup or by covering food and drinks for the audience.

### Sponsorship Options

- Venue hosting: provide the space for the meetup
- Food and beverages: sponsor snacks, drinks, or both

### Venue Requirements

- An accessible Amsterdam location that works well for an evening meetup
- Enough room for talks, audience seating, and mingling before or after the program
- Basic A/V support for presentations and demos

### Benefits

- Brand placement on the event page and meetup materials
- A short company intro during the event
- Direct face time with attendees before, during, and after the talks
- A practical way to support the Amsterdam JavaScript community and meet local engineers
